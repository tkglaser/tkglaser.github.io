Get a fixed price

Why take the uncertainty of an estimate when you can have a guaranteed fixed price upfront?

I have written about this a few times and feel quite strongly about it. Every consultant should give a fixed price upfront for every project. No guesswork, no overruns, just reliability and no risk.

The way it works is that we sit down and define what your software is going to do and what it is not going to do. I will then give you a fixed price which is exactly what the project will cost you no more no less.

Satisfaction guaranteed

I deliver quality software built to last for a long time. If you are not happy with the result, I'll give your money back.

I will involve you in the process as much or as little as you like. As soon as I have something to demo you I'll give you the opportunity to provide feedback. Once a final version has been agreed, I will hand everything over to you.

In the unlikely case that for whatever reason we cannot agree on a final version of the software, I will give your money back.

Bugfixing for life

What if you find a bug after the software has gone live?

I build quality software. If you find any bug after the software has gone live, let me know and I will fix it for free forever. Not six months, not a year, forever.

So where's the catch? There really isn't one. The only thing I would say is that I offer this for my own code only. If someone else modifies the code, my warranty is void so to speak. Also a bug isn't the same as a new feature. If there is something you'd like the software to do, let me know and I'll offer you a price.
